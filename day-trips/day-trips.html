Day trips

Curated rides that fit into a single unforgettable day.

Fort Eben-Emael: WW1 meets WW2

From the WW1 memorial in Liège to the fortress of Eben-Emael: follow the story of the German glider assault that cracked Belgium’s strongest position in May 1940. This ride links the Great War’s legacy with the daring airborne attack that opened the way for the Blitzkrieg.

Intro price: €85 per rider
Explore and book

The Grebbe Line: The Germans Are Coming!

Ride to the front lines of the May 1940 invasion where Dutch troops made a desperate 3 day stand against the Blitzkrieg. This route takes you through the preserved trenches of the Grebbeberg and the heavy concrete bunkers of the New Dutch Waterline.

Intro price: €55 per rider
Explore and book

Kornwerderzand: The Unconquered Stand

Motor across the Afsluitdijk to visit the only site where the German ground invasion was successfully halted. Explore the casemates that remained undefeated throughout the battle, surrendering only when the rest of the country laid down its arms.

Intro price: €75 per rider
Explore and book

The Hague: Paratroopers & Rockets

Uncover the daring German paratrooper raid on the Royal Family and the city's later role as a V-weapon launch site. Navigate from the historic city center to the wooded outskirts where V1 and V2 rockets were once hidden and fired toward London.

Texel: Europe’s Final Battlefield

Cross the water to the island where World War II didn't end on May 5th. This route traces the "Uprising of the Georgians," a brutal conflict between Soviet prisoners and German forces that raged weeks after the official surrender.

Operation Market Garden: A Bridge Too Far

Trace the path of the most ambitious airborne operation in history. This ride connects the drop zones of Oosterbeek with the iconic "Bridge Too Far" in Arnhem, following the heroic and tragic footsteps of Allied paratroopers.

The Atlantic Wall: Fortress Holland

Not only in Normandy! Explore the massive scale of German coastal defenses along the North Sea. This trip highlights the strategic port of IJmuiden, featuring imposing submarine pens and the sprawling bunker complexes hidden in the dunes of Wijk aan Zee.
