Welcome

Way & Witness is motorcycle tours with a difference: we ride where history happened. From WWII battlefields and bunkers in the Netherlands to the Vietcong tunnels of Vietnam and beyond, each route is a chance to witness the past and understand how it shapes the present. One unforgattable ride, one story at a time.

Your guide

Matt Szaszko has been riding since he was 14 and after 2 decades on the road he decided to turn his passion for history into Way & Witness. He has explored Northwest Europe, the Alps, Iberia, Scandinavia, India, Vietnam the Philippines, and more. He's on a mission to connect the dots between the past and the present, one ride and one story at a time.
