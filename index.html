Welcome

Way & Witness is motorcycle tours with a difference: we ride where history happened. From WWII battlefields and bunkers in the Netherlands to sacred peaks in the Himalayas and beyond, each route is a chance to witness the past and understand how it shapes the present. One ride, one story at a time.
